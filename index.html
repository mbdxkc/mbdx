our story

KC Roast Co started with a simple belief: great coffee should be accessible to everyone. Based in the heart of Kansas City, we source beans from small farms around the world and roast them in small batches to bring out their unique character.

Every bag tells a story of craft and quality—from the farmer who grew the beans to the moment you take your first sip. We're committed to sustainable sourcing and transparent practices, because we believe you deserve to know where your coffee comes from.
