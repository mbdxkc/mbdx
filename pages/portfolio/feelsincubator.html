overview

feelsIncubator is a fictional creative design studio operating at the intersection of digital design and underground aesthetics. the portfolio site needed to showcase their diverse work while maintaining a bold, contemporary presence.

the design embraces a dark, cyber-inspired aesthetic with neon accents that reflect the studio's experimental approach to visual communication. every interaction was crafted to feel intentional and immersive.
