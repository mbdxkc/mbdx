overview

the embers are a kansas city cover band bringing a whole new vibe to your good time with their versatile performances spanning contemporary hits and classic favorites. the website serves as their digital home for booking inquiries and connecting with fans.

this inviting, professional web presence showcases the band's energetic stage persona while making it easy for venues and event planners to book them.

the design lets their live performance photography speak for itself.

design process

  1. collaborated with the band to understand their vibe and booking goals
  2. curated performance photography to capture their stage energy
  3. developed a clean, modern layout that puts imagery first
  4. integrated testimonials to build credibility with potential bookers
  5. streamlined the booking flow with clear calls-to-action

design decisions

we chose a photography-forward approach for the embers—a large hero image immediately establishes their live music identity, while a grid gallery of 18+ performance shots creates an immersive visual narrative. the neutral color palette keeps focus on the band rather than competing for attention.

testimonials highlight their musical credibility, with reviewers noting the lead singer's "incredible alto/mezzo voice." clear "book us" and "follow along" calls-to-action guide visitors toward the two primary goals: securing gigs and growing their fanbase.
