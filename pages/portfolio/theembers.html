overview

The Embers are a Kansas City cover band bringing a whole new vibe to your good time. The site serves as their digital home for booking inquiries and fan connection.

The design lets their live performance photography speak for itself.

tech stack

html5 semantic markup
css3 responsive grid
javascript smooth interactions
vercel secure, lightning-fast hosting

design process

  1. discover: band vibe, booking goals, photography
  2. design: clean layout, imagery first
  3. develop: responsive build, booking form
  4. document: content updates guide
  5. deploy: vercel hosting, domain, social media assets
  6. done: live at theemberskc.com

design decisions

Photography-forward approach. A large hero image establishes their live music identity, while a grid gallery of 18+ performance shots tells their story. Neutral color palette keeps focus on the band.

Testimonials build credibility. Clear "book us" and "follow along" CTAs guide visitors toward the two goals: securing gigs and growing the fanbase.
