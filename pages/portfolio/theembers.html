overview

The Embers is a Kansas City-based cover band bringing the heat to local venues with their energetic performances of classic rock, soul, and modern hits. the website serves as their digital home for booking inquiries and show schedules.

this project is currently in the conceptual phase. we're working with the band to develop their visual identity and online presence. the goal is to create something that captures their fiery stage presence and connects them with venues and event planners across the KC metro.

planned tech stack

html5 semantic markup
css3 custom animations
javascript event calendar
netlify forms & hosting

project scope

  1. brand identity development with fire/ember motifs
  2. photo shoot coordination for band imagery
  3. show calendar with venue integration
  4. booking inquiry form for private events
  5. media kit with downloadable assets
