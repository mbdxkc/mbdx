overview

polaGram is a privacy-first iOS app that brings the nostalgic charm of instant photography to modern photo sharing. Create beautiful pola-style frames with handwritten captions, watch photos develop authentically and share memories with style.

No data collection. No analytics. No tracking. All photos stay on device.

tech stack

swiftui declarative ui
photosui modern photo picker
coremotion shake detection
vercel landing page hosting

design process

  1. discover: nostalgic instant film aesthetic, privacy-first values
  2. design: token-based system, authentic handwritten feel
  3. develop: swiftui components, cross-platform support
  4. document: comprehensive dev log and architecture docs
  5. deploy: vercel landing page, app store submission prep
  6. done: polagram.app live, app in review

photo development 20-second authentic reveal
shake to develop accelerate with motion
haptic feedback tactile shake response
handwritten captions 42-character limit

design decisions

Authentic pola aesthetic with cream frames, vintage date stamps and handwritten typography. The 20-second development animation mirrors real instant film, while shake-to-develop adds playful interaction.

Token-based design system ensures consistency across iOS, iPadOS and macOS. Champagne bubble particle effects and film grain overlays complete the nostalgic experience.
