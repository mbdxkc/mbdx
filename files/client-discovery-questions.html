Client Discovery Questions

These questions help us understand your digital presence and what you're trying to accomplish online. There are no wrong answers. Be honest about your constraints, timeline and budget. The more we understand upfront, the better we can help (or tell you if we're not the right fit).

This is not an interrogation, it's a conversation. If a question doesn't apply, skip it. If you don't know the answer yet, that's useful information too.

Your Business Basics

  • What do you do, and who do you do it for?
  • How long have you been doing this?
  • What makes you different from others doing similar work?
  • How do people currently find you or hear about you?

Your Digital Goals and Challenges

  • Why are you thinking about your digital presence right now?
    • What triggered this? New opportunity? Current approach not working?
  • What's not working about your current situation?
  • What would success look like three months after we finish?
    • Be specific: more inquiries? easier to update? people understanding what you do?
  • If you do nothing, what happens?

Your Current Digital Presence

  • Where do people find you online right now? (website, social media, Google, directories?)
  • Do you have a website? If yes, what's the URL?
  • What platform is it on? (Squarespace, WordPress, custom, etc.)
  • What do you like about your current site? What frustrates you?
  • Are you active on social media? Which platforms?
  • Do you have a Google Business Profile? Is it up to date?
  • Do you send emails or newsletters to customers?
  • Do you update it yourself, or does someone else handle it?
  • What have customers or visitors said about your site?

What You're Trying to Accomplish

  • What do you want your digital presence to do for you?
    • Get more customers? Build credibility? Showcase work? Take bookings?
  • Where do most of your customers/clients come from right now?
  • What's working well in how you currently connect with people online?
  • What feels like a waste of time or isn't working?
  • What's the most important thing people should be able to do on your site?
  • Do you want to manage and update content yourself?
  • What pages or sections do you definitely need?
  • Do you need bilingual content (English/Spanish)?

Your People and How They Find You

  • Who is your typical customer/client/visitor?
    • Don't say "everyone." Pick your most common type of person.
  • How do people usually discover you?
  • What's the typical path someone takes before becoming a customer?
  • What questions do people always ask you?
  • What would make someone choose you over a competitor?

Brand and Visual Identity

  • Show us 2-3 examples of digital presence you like. What appeals to you?
    • We're looking for patterns: clean vs busy, colorful vs minimal
  • Do you have a logo? Brand colors? Fonts?
  • How would you describe your business's personality in three words?
  • What should people feel when they interact with you online?

Content and Assets

  • Do you have photos you can use? (products, space, team, work samples?)
  • Who's writing the content? You or us?
    • If you, we can provide a template. If us, that's a separate scope.
  • What content do you already have that we can use or adapt?

Tools and Technical Setup

  • What digital tools are you currently using? (scheduling, email marketing, payment processing, etc.)
  • What's working well? What's frustrating or confusing?
  • Do you own a domain name? What is it?
  • Do you have hosting? (or should we recommend something?)
  • Do you need email addresses at your domain?
  • What systems need to work together? (booking, e-commerce, email, CRM?)

Timeline and Constraints

  • When do you need this done?
    • Is this "ASAP" or "sometime this year"? Hard deadline or flexible?
  • What's driving the timeline? (event, season, opportunity?)
  • How much time can you dedicate to this project?

Budget Reality Check

  • What's your budget range for this project?
    • Honesty helps us both. If it doesn't match our rates, we can recommend other options.
  • Are you open to a phased approach? (launch with essentials, add features later)
  • What's your monthly budget for ongoing costs?
    • Usually $10-$50 a month depending on platform.

Ongoing Management

  • Do you want to manage your digital presence yourself, or have someone help?
  • How much time can you realistically spend on digital stuff each week?
  • How comfortable are you with technology and learning new tools?
  • Do you need training or documentation on managing things?

The Honest Questions

  • Have you worked with someone on your digital presence before? How did it go?
  • What are you worried about with this project?
  • What would make you feel confident that this is going well?
  • Is there anything we haven't asked that we should know?

What Happens Next

After we talk through these, we'll send you a clear proposal with scope, timeline and cost. If it's a fit, great. If not, we'll be honest about that too and can point you toward better options.
